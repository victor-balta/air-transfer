# AirTransfer 📡

**Instantly transfer files from phone to Mac over Wi-Fi.**
No internet required. Private, fast, and secure.

## 🚀 Quick Start

1. **Clone & Start**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/victor-balta/air-transfer.git
   cd air-transfer
   ./start.sh
   ```

2. **Connect Phone**
   - Scan the **QR Code** that appears in your terminal.
   - Or open the URL (e.g., `http://192.168.1.x:5002`) in your phone's browser.

3. **Send Files**
   - Tap to upload or drag & drop.
   - Files are saved to `~/Downloads` on your Mac.

---

## ✨ Features
- **Native Experience**: Install as a PWA on your phone home screen.
- **Notifications**: Get a sound and popup on your Mac when files arrive.
- **Share Target**: Share directly from Android Gallery/Files apps.
